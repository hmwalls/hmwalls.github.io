Our Story

My dad has taught me a lot of things. First, he instilled in me the importance of being an independent woman. From how to change a flat tire to how to order beer in Spanish, he taught me how to be self-sufficient. Two, he always taught me to be prepared for everything. Growing up we had flashlights everywhere and always had a clear exit path in every room just in case we had to leave in a hurry. Third and most importantly, he taught me to have a good sense of humor and not take myself too seriously. Instead of swearing “oh shit” he would say (and still says) “oh sugarbeets.” Sugarbeets is inspired by my dad. Sugarbeets is all about independent women being prepared for life’s unexpected moments all while having a good time and enjoying life. We’re making sure that you’re covered in your “oh sugarbeets” moments. ;)

Frequently Asked Questions

+ Can I return Sugarbeets?

You can return items within 30 days of shipment. Only return bags in which the contents have not been used or replaced will be accepted for a full return.

+ Why are doing pre-orders?

We’re bootstrapping Sugarbeets so that we can build a financially healthy company to pursue our social mission of empowering women. With that said, Sugarbeets requires significant inventory, so by taking pre-orders we’re able to bootstrap our inventory supply.

+ When can I expect my order?

We expect to ship our first Sugarbeets bags in September 2014.

+ Where does the name come from? 

Sugarbeets comes from a saying Kristen’s dad says - instead of swearing he says “oh sugarbeets!” Read all the details in the full story.

+ Can I make suggestion on products in the bag?

There are so many possibilities so we can’t make any promises, but we’d love to hear your suggestions! Let us know what you’re thinking.

+ Why shouldn’t I just make my own?

Of course we’re biased, but buying a Sugarbeets bag is the way to go. When you buy a Sugarbeets bag you’re supporting US manufacturers and eco-friendly products. Most importantly, you’re investing in the mission to empower women.

+ Can I get my product into a Sugarbeets bag?

We’re always looking for eco-friendly products. If your company makes items that you think would go perfect in a day-to-day survival kit, drop us a line.

+ I would like to sell Sugarbeets in my store. Do you provide wholesale pricing?

We’re still working on our pricing. Let us know you’re interested in carrying Sugarbeets and we’ll get back to you when we’re ready.
